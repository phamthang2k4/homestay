ChiPa Homestay

K10/1A Pham Van Nghi Thach Gian Ward - Thanh Khe District - Da Nang City, Da Nang, Vietnam

Conveniently located in Da Nang, ChiPa Home offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, free parking and room service.

The property is located around 2 km from Indochina Riverside Mall, 2.2 km from Love Pier and 2.4 km from Song Han Bridge.

The property has a shared kitchen, concierge services and organizes tours for guests.

Rooms at the guest house are equipped with a wardrobe. All rooms have a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries.

Popular attractions near ChiPa Home include Thanh Binh Beach, Cham Museum and Han Market. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 2 km from the room.
