TEEPEE Homestay

54 street Ngo Thi Sy, Da Lat, Viet Nam

Located in Da Lat in the Lam Dong region with Bao Dai's Summer Palace nearby, Nấp ở TEEPEE homestay provides accommodation with free private parking.

Some accommodation includes a terrace with mountain views, a fully equipped kitchen and a private bathroom with bidet.

Guests at the homestay can enjoy an Asian breakfast.

Nấp ở TEEPEE homestay offers an outdoor fireplace..

A an indoor play area is also available at the accommodation, while guests can also relax in the garden.

Lam Vien Square is 3.3 km from Nấp ở TEEPEE homestay, while Xuan Huong Lake is 3.5 km away. The nearest airport is Lien Khuong Airport, 29 km from the homestay.
