Haus Homestay

Thon 3/2b, Lien Son, Luong Son, Hoa Binh, Viet Nam

Haus Homestay Hòa Bình - Venue Travel has mountain views, free WiFi and free private parking, situated in Hòa Bình, 42 km from My Dinh Stadium.

Fitted with a patio, the units offer air conditioning and feature a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with bidet and slippers. Some units have a seating area and/or a balcony.

The villa offers an outdoor fireplace.

In addition to a year-round outdoor pool, LaVa Haus Homestay Hòa Bình - Venue Travel also features a a children's playground and shared lounge.

Vincom Center Nguyen Chi Thanh is 42 km from the accommodation, while Vietnam Fine Arts Museum is 43 km from the property. The nearest airport is Noi Bai International Airport, 69 km from LaVa Haus Homestay Hòa Bình - Venue Travel.
